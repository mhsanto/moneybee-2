Target Customer Groups

Our SME Loan is built for Malaysian businesses that are ready to grow, stabilize, or optimize operations—especially those who may have difficulty getting traditional loans easily. These include

Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)

Businesses wanting to scale

Underserved SMEs

Existing SMEs aiming for modernization

investing in digital transformation, automation, technology upgrades.

Bumiputera

often eligible for certain government-backed financing schemes.

Eligibility

Typical criteria in Malaysia’s SME lending market

Business registration

  • Registered with SSM (or relevant authority), valid license.

Business operating period

  • Usually at least 1 year, sometimes 2–3 years (some banks require 3 years)

Revenue threshold

  • Depends on lender; e.g. for smaller SME loans, minimal revenue acceptable.

Profitability

  • Financial statements / bank statements to show viability.

Credit

  • Business and/or owner’s credit history (CTOS, CCRIS) will be considered.

Ownership

  • Malaysia-based, majority Malaysian ownership in many schemes.

Collateral

  • Required for certain amounts (e.g. property, equipment) depending on lender.

Our AI first screens your profile before forwarding to lenders, minimizing rejections and wasted effort.
